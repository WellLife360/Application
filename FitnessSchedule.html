Fitness Schedule Guide

When creating your own fitness schedule, itâ€™s important to include a variety of exercises that target different muscle groups. Below are some tips to guide you in making a balanced workout schedule:

  • Cardio Exercises: Include at least 2-3 cardio sessions per week (e.g., running, cycling, swimming).
  • Strength Training: Incorporate 2-3 strength training sessions to target major muscle groups (e.g., squats, deadlifts, push-ups).

  • Rest Days: Ensure you include rest or active recovery days to allow your muscles to repair and grow.
